WELCOME TO RABIN's SITE
It's the site that tells about my school
Allow me to introduce myself.

I'm Rabinchandra, a student of JRD Pathseekers' Academy and a former student of Padma Ratna English School, Kakching. Today, I'm telling about my Hr. Secondary School in which I'm studying class 11 now.

Is it a good School?

Yess!! it's a really good Hr. Secondary school where a student, after passing class X examination, can study class XI and XII Science.

Are teachers good?

Absolutely!! The teachers are really good and very friendly. We can ask questions to the teachers without any frightening and they help us to build up our character.

Is there any co-curricular activies?

Yes!! In addition to that, while doing assembly a seleted student should give a thought of the day in either English or Manipuri which helps in personality development.
